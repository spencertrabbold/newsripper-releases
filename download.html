News.Ripper 3.8.1

Reliability update for safe newsroom video downloading.

Download NewsRipper 3.8.1 Installer

What changed

  • YouTube videos that began as live streams (city council meetings, press conferences) now download — they previously failed with "This video is not available."
  • Cancel now works during the "merging audio and video" step of large downloads and cleans up all partial files.
  • Tricky news sites work more often: the site analyzer presses play inside embedded frames, dismisses cookie pop-ups (never accepting on your behalf), and wakes up lazy-loading players.
  • Full disks are explained in plain English, and downloads refuse to start when space is critically low.
  • Your cookies file is protected from damage; if it's ever empty or broken, Settings says so clearly.
  • Includes everything from 3.8.0: hardware-accelerated conversions, parallel downloads, the self-updating downloader engine, plain-English errors, and more.
