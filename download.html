News.Ripper 3.8.0

Performance and reliability update for safe newsroom video downloading.

Download NewsRipper 3.8.0 Installer

What changed

  • Much faster conversions: hardware-accelerated video encoding when your computer supports it, with automatic software fallback. Typical converted downloads finish several times faster.
  • Faster downloads: video segments now download in parallel.
  • Self-updating downloader engine: fixes for individual sites can arrive between app releases. Updates are user-initiated, checksum-verified, and one-click revertable.
  • Plain-English error messages with recovery tips.
  • Completed downloads show how long each step took (measured on your computer only).
  • If the app ever crashes, it leaves a clearly named CRASH file in the log folder to share.
  • New "Report a problem" button: opens your log folder and a pre-addressed email draft.
  • New "Prepare site analyzer now" button to pre-download the fallback browser on your schedule.
  • Hardening: cookies-path validation with visible errors, pasted page source capped at 10 MB.
