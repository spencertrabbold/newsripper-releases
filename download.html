What changed

  • Updated yt-dlp to 2026.6.9.

  • Neutralizes dangerous filename segments like .exe, .bat, .cmd, .ps1, .url, and .lnk.
  • Validates downloaded and final media files using file signatures.
  • Adds Windows Zone.Identifier / Mark-of-the-Web to final MP4 files.
