News.Ripper 3.9.2

A refreshed look for safe newsroom video downloading.

What changed

  • More sites download successfully — when a site's video can't be read the usual way, News.Ripper now automatically tries its built-in browser analyzer instead of giving up (fixes some local-news and network-affiliate pages, e.g. NBC-owned stations).
  • A refreshed, calm, ocean-inspired design built for the newsroom, with the paste-and-download flow front and center.
  • Every download now shows its stage at a glance: Downloading, Merging audio & video, Converting to broadcast MP4, and Ready.
  • Settings moved into a slide-over panel that stays out of your way.
  • New app icon and launch screen.
  • Includes everything from 3.8.x: hardware-accelerated conversions, parallel downloads, the self-updating downloader engine, YouTube live-stream (VOD) support, plain-English errors, and disk-space and cookie-file safeguards.
